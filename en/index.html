Architecture as a couple

Colleagues and married, passionate about the same field. After meeting at the Cranbrook Academy of Art in 1940, they shared the same destiny, and their contribution to the world of design and architecture is still felt today.

Works in the Eames style

From American-style houses to church buildings, the couple have been able to get to grips with different structural styles. That said, it's one of their strengths that goes hand in hand with their technical expertise.
